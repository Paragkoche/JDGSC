About our GDSC

Google Developer Student Club of J D College of Engineering and Management was established in the year 2022 by Mr. Shreyash Giri. This club is the most technically active club of the college and is always active in the conduction of any tech-based event whenever given an opportunity. GDSC JDCOEM Targets to spread technical awareness and help students build projects and grow and learn in the developer space.

Great things about Google DSC

Networking.

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) play a crucial role in student network growth by providing a platform for like-minded individuals to connect.

Technical Skills.

We significantly contribute to students' technical skill growth by organizing hands-on workshops, coding challenges, and tech talks.

Hands-On-Experience.

Offering students valuable hands-on experience by organizing hackathons, coding bootcamps, and project development initiatives.
